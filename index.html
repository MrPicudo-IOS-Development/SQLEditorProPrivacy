Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

SQL Editor Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Miguel Torres, an independent developer based in Mexico. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you use SQL Editor Pro and how that data is handled.

Summary

SQL Editor Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the experience.

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This helps us identify which features are most used and improve the overall experience. Firebase Analytics may collect the following anonymous data:

  • App usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • Crash and performance data
  • Country-level location (derived from IP address, which is not stored)

This data is anonymous, aggregated, and cannot be used to identify you personally. Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

For more information on how Google handles this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase's Privacy documentation.

In-App Purchases

SQL Editor Pro offers in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit framework. All purchase transactions, billing information, and payment processing are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID.

Data Storage & Security

All your learning progress and app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party, with the exception of the anonymous analytics data described above.

Children's Privacy

SQL Editor Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction). Since we do not collect personal data from any user, the app is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) — anonymous usage analytics
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchase processing

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you wish to reset your anonymous analytics data, you can reinstall the app or disable analytics data sharing in your device settings under Settings → Privacy → Analytics.
